My day

Present Simple

Usually, I get up at 7 o'clock. Then I take a shower and have my coffee with milk and a big sandwich, or two small sandwiches. After that I dress myself and go to work where I teach children, because I'm a primary school teacher. After work I go home and have my lunch. Then I cookfood and do private tutoring sometimes. In the evening, I often study English, have a bath, clean my teeth and go to bed.
